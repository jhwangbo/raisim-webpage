Licensing Process
========================================

Commercial users must contact info.raisim@gmail.com to obtain a commercial client ID prior to proceeding with the instructions below.

An **activation key** is required to operate RaiSim.
Activation keys may be requested via the **RaiSim License Request Form**:

`Go to the RaiSim License Request Form <https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScNL0vbZPDNS93L6Jv6fgR51WTsvXxfhnVOtKDVRdAmHIoG4w/viewform?usp=sf_link>`__

Upon submission and review of the request, an activation key will be issued.

Refer to the included examples for instructions on using the activation key.
Further details are available in the Installation section.
For licensing inquiries, please contact info.raisim@gmail.com.

Available License Types:

* Commercial License (`SLA <https://drive.google.com/file/d/1IAHbS8mXTCyVAnMikp3WyT0cCqFCxTZw/view?usp=sharing>`__): 1000 USD per person per year.
* Academic License (`SLA <https://drive.google.com/file/d/10W_9H8Ur37B6GllR0Qb3JQyj7Fh-AVlx/view?usp=sharing>`__): Free of charge.
* Trial License (`SLA <https://drive.google.com/file/d/1-fUZx2_h9UO3KcePWRjRz-itiypHORp2/view?usp=sharing>`__): Free of charge, valid for one month.

Purchases of three or more commercial licenses include a master key valid for use on any machine.
